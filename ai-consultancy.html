Solving Critical Business Problems With AI Domains

AI Consulting Development and Practice

Our Consultants conduct deep research into your problem to refine requirements, then collect data and build an efficient solution to fulfill your Business Requirements and provide additional insights that will help you make better decisions in the future.

Our Approach

Planning

A team meeting is conducted, the problem is brainstormed and the team finalizes a domain to utilize in order to come up with a solution.

Data Collection

Existing data allows us to measure the effectiveness of the proposed solution. If a data set does not exist, we brainstorm alternative ways to start collecting it or choose another way forward.

Training

A model is trained using the data collected in the previous step. The algorithm is tweaked until the required output is achieved and processes are introduced for further improvement.

Deploying & Testing

The finalized model is deployed and undergoes numerous testing protocols to ensure efficiency and robustness.

Testing and Validation

After the launch of the MVP, the hypothesis backing the MVP are then tested, and feedback is gathered from actual users as to make the iterative betterment of the product certain.

Feedback Loops and Iterations

The feedback incorporation, testing and validation loops are then catered till another featured needs to be rolled out. The team is readily available for any support that may be required during this time.
