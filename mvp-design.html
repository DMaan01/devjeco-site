Build and Validate an MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

MVP Design and Development Lifecycle

We specialise in mapping out the trajectory of your product idea and providing guidance in selecting the essential technological domains, while ensuring alignment with your product's roadmap. Our focus is on facilitating a proper and well-paced scaling strategy, avoiding premature expansion and enabling sustainable growth for your business.

Our Approach

Defining the MVP with a PRD

We establish a comprehensive Product Requirements Document that encapsulates the products' story, features, and purpose, serving as the authoritative reference point throughout the entire product lifecycle. This document encompasses the problem statement, ideation details, as well as all relevant assumptions, constraints, and key metrics associated with the product.

Information Architecture

We meticulously plan and establish the product's structure, content hierarchy, and essential Journey Maps, which serve as vital blueprints for outlining how users will interact with the product, ensuring a seamless and successful journey towards achieving their goals.

Wireframes and Prototypes

Following the comprehensive definition and organisation of the product's structure and content hierarchy, we proceed to conceptualise ideas and construct wireframes. These wireframes are subsequently validated by stakeholders and users. Upon successful validation, a high-fidelity prototype is delivered to the development team, marking the initiation of the development phase.

Development Pipeline

The development pipeline is initiated by the Development Team, carefully configuring environments, repositories, and establishing an efficient CI/CD process. This enables rapid iteration deployment with minimal effort, ensuring ease of scalability.

Testing and Validation

Following the MVP launch, the underlying hypotheses are rigorously tested, and valuable feedback is collected from real users to ensure the iterative improvement and enhancement of the product.

Feedback Loops and Iterations

The feedback incorporation, testing, and validation loops are diligently managed until the rollout of additional features becomes necessary. Our team remains readily available to provide any necessary support during this phase.
